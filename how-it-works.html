How It Works

Step 1: Fill Out the Form

Tell us everything about your child — their name, age, personality, and even favorite animals!

Step 2: Check Your Email

You’ll receive a story summary, book number, and a link to purchase your personalized book.

Step 3: Receive Your Book

Enjoy your custom hardcover story delivered right to your door — made for cozy reading nights.

Step 4: Try Again or Continue

Not in love with the summary? Try again free! Or use your book number to get the next one in the series.
