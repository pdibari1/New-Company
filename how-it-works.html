How It Works

Fill out the form

with as much information as possible.

You’ll receive an email

with a story summary, book number and link to purchase.

Click the link

and you’ll receive your personalized hardcover book in the mail.

If you don’t like the summary, just try again for a whole new summary.
The book number will allow you to purchase the next book in your series!
